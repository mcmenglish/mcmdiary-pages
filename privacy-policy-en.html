MCM Diary ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application.

1. Information We Collect

We collect the following information to provide our services:

  • Email Address: Used for account authentication and login purposes only.
  • Diary Entries: The diary content you create through conversations with our AI.
  • Chat Messages: Conversations with our AI assistant used to generate your diary entries.

We do NOT collect:

  • Your name
  • Gender
  • Age or date of birth
  • Location data
  • Phone number
  • Photos or media (unless you explicitly share them)

2. How We Use Your Information

Your information is used to:

  • Create and manage your account
  • Generate personalized AI diary entries based on your conversations
  • Store and display your diary entries
  • Improve our services

3. Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services:

  • Firebase (Google): For user authentication and secure data storage. Firebase Privacy Policy
  • OpenAI: For AI-powered conversations and diary generation. Your chat messages are processed by OpenAI to generate responses. OpenAI Privacy Policy
  • Google AdMob: For displaying advertisements. AdMob may collect device information for ad personalization. Google Privacy Policy

4. Data Storage and Security

Your data is stored securely using Firebase Cloud Firestore. We implement appropriate security measures to protect your personal information. Your diary entries are associated with your account and are not shared with other users.

5. Data Retention

Your data is retained as long as your account is active. You can delete individual diary entries at any time. If you wish to delete your account and all associated data, please contact us.

6. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal data
  • Delete your diary entries
  • Request deletion of your account
  • Opt out of personalized advertising

7. Children's Privacy

MCM Diary is not intended for children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last Updated" date.
