üìö Frequently Asked Questions

Q: How do I create a diary entry?

Tap on any date in the calendar, then chat with our AI about your day. When you're ready, tap "Done" to generate your diary entry.

Q: Can I write my diary in different languages?

Yes! MCM Diary supports English, Korean, and Japanese. You can also choose to write your diary in English (with key expressions explained in your native language) to help learn English.

Q: How do I delete a diary entry?

Open the diary entry you want to delete, scroll down, and tap "Delete Diary". You can also rewrite a diary by tapping "Rewrite Diary".

Q: Is my diary data private?

Yes! Your diary entries are stored securely and are only accessible by you. We do not share your personal diary content with anyone.

Q: How do I change the app language?

Go to Settings (gear icon) and tap on "Language" to select your preferred language.

Q: What are "Key Expressions"?

When you write your diary in English, we highlight useful English expressions and explain them in your native language. This helps you learn new vocabulary while journaling!

Q: How do I reset my password?

On the login screen, tap "Forgot Password?" and enter your email address. We'll send you a link to reset your password.

Q: How do I delete my account?

Please contact us at master1000online@gmail.com with your request to delete your account and all associated data.
